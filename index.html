Tillman A. Riewe

Professional Engineer, 1st Lt., Husband & Father

Tillman A. Riewe sitting with his youngest son David. circa 1968

Here's a timeline of Tillman Riewe's Life:

  • 1928 - Born in Pottsville, Texas
  • 1946 - Leaves his family's farm to attend the Texas A&M
  • 1950 - Graduated with Bachelor of Science from Texas A&M
  • 1950 - U.S. Army as 2nd and 1st Lt.
  • 1951 - Married Lottie Sue Chastain
  • 1951 - Stationed in Germany
  • 1954 - Completed Masters at Texas A&M
  • 1954 - Joined Forrest & Cotton Engineering Firm
  • 1964 - Became Vice President of National Soil Services
  • 1981 - Listed in Who's Who In The South and Southwest
  • 1982 - Became Vice President of Rone Engineers
  • 1984 - Died after year struggle with lukemia
  • 1984 - Posthumously Awarded Engineer of the year
